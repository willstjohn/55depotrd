Details

55 Depot Road is a move-in ready Nantucket Colonial situated on 2.12 acres and located .5 miles from the charming East Boxford village. The East Boxford village is home to Boxford Community Kitchen, Cole Elementary School, Boxford Commons athletic complex and part of the desirable Laurel Hollow neighborhood. Less than 5 minutes to I-95.


White and walnut custom cabinetry in the updated eat-in chef’s kitchen featuring appliances from Sub- Zero, Miele and Capital. Cathedral ceiling family room with custom built-ins and wood burning insert adjoins a bright sunroom featuring radiant heat and eight large windows to capture the entertainer’s dream back yard. Finishing the first floor is an updated laundry/pantry area with custom cabinets, a second refrigerator and abundant storage, large living room with gas fireplace, formal dining room featuring Phillip Jeffries wallpaper and custom window treatments, updated large powder room with shiplap, custom closets and cubbies in the back hall mudroom area. Hardwood floors with custom stain throughout.


Upstairs there are 4 bedrooms, office with custom cabinetry, built-ins and custom stained hardwood floors. Both the primary bathroom and hall bathroom have been updated with custom cabinetry and contemporary finishes. Radiant heat in primary bathroom with freestanding slipper tub. One of the bedrooms is located over the garage with an en-suite updated bathroom, 3rd zone heat and a rear staircase to access it.


Finished lower level with hardwood floors, 4 zones of radiant heat with a full bathroom, second media area with 7.1 surround sound, gym area and guest sleeping quarters.


Step outside and you will find an 18x36 heated salt water gunite pool with PebbleTec finish and integrated spa and auto cover. Custom outdoor kitchen with appliances from Lynx featuring separate double gas burners, 48” gas grill and cocktail pro. Ice maker, beverage center and beadboard cabinets complete the kitchen. Hardscape area made of bluestone and Vermont granite with plenty of space for loungers, dining and sitting. Custom pool house with 1/2 bath, sitting area, TV/outdoor speakers and outdoor shower.


The home was built in 1998, but features many updates including new furnace 2019, stainless steel water heater 2018, roof 2012, windows 2010 and exterior doors 2020.


Mature landscaping and professional landscape lighting welcome you home. 13 zone irrigation, in ground dog fence and fenced-in vegetable garden complete the back yard. The home also features a large leveled side yard for outdoor hockey rink/sports. Direct access to the Boxford Trails Association trail network for mountain biking and walks with furry friends.


Asking Price: $1,825,000
